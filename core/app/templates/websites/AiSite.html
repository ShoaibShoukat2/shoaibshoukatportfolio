AI-Powered Car Detection Project

A smart AI solution for detecting vehicle road status, with manual verification options for precise results.

This project leverages artificial intelligence to analyze images of cars uploaded by users. The AI model detects if a car is on the road or not and displays the initial verification status as either "Pending" or "Accepted," based on the AI's assessment.

Admins can manually review the AI-generated status. If the AI makes an error, the admin can override it, updating the status to either accept or reject the recommendation. The final status—whether AI-verified or admin-reviewed—is then displayed to the user.

AI Detection Process

The AI model analyzes the uploaded car image and identifies if the car is on a road. This initial detection status is displayed to the user as either "Accepted" or "Pending."

Admin Verification

Admins can manually review and verify the AI-generated results, ensuring accuracy. If the AI's assessment is incorrect, the admin has the option to override the status.

Status Display

The final status, verified by AI or manually by an admin, is shown to the user. This clear display allows users to see if further verification was required for complete accuracy.
